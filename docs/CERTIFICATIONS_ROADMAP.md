## 1. Certification Categories

| Category | Description | Examples |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| **Regulatory licences** | Mandatory government/regulator approvals to operate | ACL, AFSL, NMLS, FCA authorisation |
| **Industry memberships** | Voluntary (but commercially essential) industry bodies | ERC (UK), MFAA (AU), MBA (US) |
| **Professional certifications** | Individual qualifications for staff giving advice | RG 146 (AU), Series 7 (US), CeMAP (UK) |
| **Technology certifications** | Platform security and data handling standards | SOC 2, ISO 27001, PCI DSS |
| **Product approvals** | Regulator sign-off on the EPM product itself | ASIC product ruling, FCA product approval |

## 2. Australia

### 2.1 Mandatory Licences

| Licence | Issuer | Purpose | Timeline | Prerequisite |
|---------|--------|---------|----------|-------------|
| **Australian Credit Licence (ACL)** | ASIC | Engage in credit activities (EPM is a credit product) | 3-6 months | Fit & proper persons, PI insurance, compliance arrangements |
| **Australian Financial Services Licence (AFSL)** | ASIC | Provide financial product advice (if investment component classified as financial product) | 6-12 months | Competency (RG 146), PI insurance, compliance framework, responsible managers |
| **ABN / ACN** | ASIC / ATO | Business registration | 1 week | Standard |

### 2.2 Conditional: AFSL vs ACL Only

**If ASIC classifies EPM as a credit product only:** ACL sufficient.  
**If ASIC classifies EPM as a financial product (managed investment scheme):** AFSL also required.

**Recommended approach:** Apply for both simultaneously. The AFSL application is longer but running in parallel saves 6+ months.

### 2.3 Industry Memberships

| Membership | Body | Why | Cost |
|-----------|------|-----|------|
| MFAA (Mortgage & Finance Association of Australia) | Industry | Broker network access, credibility | ~$2,000/year |
| FBAA (Finance Brokers Association of Australia) | Industry | Alternative broker network | ~$1,500/year |
| AFCA (Australian Financial Complaints Authority) | Mandatory | External dispute resolution (EDR) — required for ACL/AFSL | ~$3,500/year + per-complaint levy |

### 2.4 Professional Certifications (Staff)

| Certification | For Whom | Requirement | Provider |
|--------------|----------|-------------|----------|
| RG 146 (Tier 1 — General Advice) | All customer-facing staff | Mandatory for AFSL | Kaplan, TAFE |
| RG 146 (Tier 2 — Personal Advice) | Advisers recommending EPM variant | Mandatory if providing personal advice | Kaplan, TAFE |
| Diploma of Finance (FNS50322) | Responsible Managers on ACL | Mandatory (or equivalent experience) | Various RTOs |
| FASEA exam (Financial Adviser exam) | If classified as financial adviser | Mandatory post-FASEA reforms | ASIC-approved providers |

### 2.5 Product Approval

| Approval | Detail | Timeline |
|----------|--------|----------|
| **ASIC no-action letter** | Confirm EPM classification (credit vs financial product) | 3-6 months (engage early) |
| **Target Market Determination (TMD)** | DDO requirement — define target market for EPM | Before distribution |
| **Product Disclosure Statement (PDS)** | If AFSL required | Before distribution |
| **Credit Guide** | ACL requirement | Before engaging with consumers |

## 3. United States

### 3.1 Mandatory Licences

| Licence | Issuer | Purpose | Timeline | Prerequisite |
|---------|--------|---------|----------|-------------|
| **NMLS registration** | NMLS (state-administered) | Mortgage lender/broker registration | 2-4 months per state | Background checks, financial statements, surety bond |
| **State mortgage lender licence** | Each state regulator | Authority to originate mortgages in that state | 2-6 months per state | Net worth requirements, surety bond, designated responsible individual |

### 3.2 State-by-State Licensing (Phase 1)

| State | Regulator | Licence Type | Surety Bond | Net Worth | Timeline |
|-------|-----------|-------------|-------------|-----------|----------|
| **California** | DFPI (Dept of Financial Protection & Innovation) | CFL Licence or CRMLA | $25,000 | $250,000 | 3-4 months |
| **New York** | DFS (Dept of Financial Services) | Mortgage Banker Licence | $50,000 | $500,000 | 4-6 months |
| **Florida** | OFR (Office of Financial Regulation) | Mortgage Lender Licence | $25,000 | $250,000 | 2-3 months |
| **Arizona** | DIFI | Mortgage Banker Licence | $25,000 | $100,000 | 2-3 months |

### 3.3 Federal Considerations

| Requirement | Authority | Detail |
|-------------|-----------|--------|
| **SEC exemption** | SEC | If EPM is classified as a security (Howey test risk), must register or qualify for exemption. Engage securities counsel immediately. |
| **CFPB compliance** | CFPB | TILA, RESPA, ECOA disclosures. No separate licence but compliance is mandatory. |
| **FHA/HUD** | HUD | If seeking FHA endorsement (unlikely for Phase 1 but valuable long-term for HECM-like treatment). |
| **BSA/AML** | FinCEN | Register as MSB if applicable. File SARs, maintain AML program. |

### 3.4 Industry Memberships

| Membership | Body | Why | Cost |
|-----------|------|-----|------|
| MBA (Mortgage Bankers Association) | Industry | Credibility, lobbying access, conferences | ~$5,000/year |
| NRMLA (National Reverse Mortgage Lenders Association) | Reverse mortgage specific | EPM is adjacent — valuable for positioning | ~$3,000/year |
| BBB (Better Business Bureau) | Consumer trust | Rating and accreditation | ~$500/year |

### 3.5 Professional Certifications (Staff)

| Certification | For Whom | Requirement | Provider |
|--------------|----------|-------------|----------|
| NMLS-approved pre-licensing education | All loan originators | 20 hours federal + state hours | NMLS-approved providers |
| SAFE Act exam | All loan originators | National + state component | NMLS |
| Series 63/66 | If SEC registration required | State securities licence | FINRA |
| CRMP (Certified Reverse Mortgage Professional) | Senior advisers | Voluntary but valuable | NRMLA |

## 4. New Zealand

### 4.1 Mandatory Licences

| Licence | Issuer | Purpose | Timeline | Prerequisite |
|---------|--------|---------|----------|-------------|
| **Financial Service Provider (FSP) registration** | Companies Office | Registration to provide financial services | 2-4 weeks | NZ company, AML compliance |
| **Financial Advice Provider (FAP) licence** | FMA | If providing financial advice to consumers | 3-6 months | Competency standards, PI insurance, complaints process |
| **Dispute Resolution Scheme membership** | FDRS / FSCL / Banking Ombudsman | External dispute resolution (mandatory for FSPs) | 2-4 weeks | Annual fee |

### 4.2 Conditional Licences

| Licence | When Required |
|---------|--------------|
| **Managed Investment Scheme (MIS) manager licence** | If EPM portfolio is classified as a managed fund offered to consumers |
| **Derivatives Issuer licence** | If NNEG or hedging instruments are offered to NZ consumers |

**Recommendation:** Structure EPM so the consumer receives loan advances only (Model B). This avoids MIS and derivatives licensing — simpler, faster to market.

### 4.3 Industry Memberships

| Membership | Body | Why | Cost |
|-----------|------|-----|------|
| Financial Services Federation (FSF) | Industry | Responsible lending code signatory | ~$2,000/year |
| NZBA (NZ Bankers' Association) | Banking | If partnering with NZ banks | Varies |
| Dispute resolution (FDRS recommended) | Mandatory | EDR scheme | ~$1,500/year + levies |

### 4.4 Professional Certifications (Staff)

| Certification | For Whom | Requirement |
|--------------|----------|-------------|
| NZ Certificate in Financial Services (Level 5) | All advisers | Mandatory competency standard under Financial Markets Conduct Act |
| Code of Professional Conduct compliance | All financial advice providers | FMA Code Standards |
| AML/CFT training | All relevant staff | Mandatory under AML/CFT Act 2009 |

## 5. United Kingdom

### 5.1 Mandatory Licences

| Licence | Issuer | Purpose | Timeline | Prerequisite |
|---------|--------|---------|----------|-------------|
| **FCA Authorisation** | FCA | Permission to conduct regulated activities (mortgage lending/advising) | 6-12 months | Business plan, capital requirements, compliance arrangements, fit & proper persons |
| **FCA Permission: home finance mediation** | FCA | Specifically for lifetime mortgage advice | Included in authorisation | Qualification requirements (CeMAP + ER01) |
| **FCA Permission: mortgage lending** | FCA | If lending directly (not just advising) | Included in authorisation | Prudential requirements (MIPRU) |
| **ICO Registration** | ICO | Data protection registration (UK GDPR) | 2-4 weeks | Annual fee £40-£2,900 based on size |

### 5.2 ERC Membership (Commercially Essential)

| Requirement | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| **Equity Release Council membership** | Not legally mandatory, but commercially essential — 95%+ of UK equity release is via ERC members |
| **ERC Standards** | NNEG guarantee, right to remain, advised sales only, independent legal advice |
| **Application process** | Demonstrate compliance with all ERC standards, PI insurance, complaints process |
| **Timeline** | 2-3 months after FCA authorisation |
| **Cost** | Membership fee (varies by size) + annual compliance levy |

**Without ERC membership, FutureProof will lack credibility in the UK market.** Advisers will not recommend non-ERC products.

### 5.3 Professional Certifications (Staff)

| Certification | For Whom | Requirement | Provider |
|--------------|----------|-------------|----------|
| **CeMAP (Certificate in Mortgage Advice and Practice)** | All mortgage advisers | FCA minimum qualification | IFS / London Institute of Banking & Finance |
| **ER01 (Equity Release)** | Advisers recommending EPM | FCA requires ER-specific qualification | IFS |
| **CF1/CF6 (Approved Persons)** | Senior managers, compliance | FCA Senior Managers & Certification Regime (SM&CR) | Various |
| **SPS (Statement of Professional Standing)** | All advisers annually | FCA ongoing requirement | Accredited bodies |

### 5.4 PRA Requirements (If Lender Entity)

If FutureProof operates a UK lending entity (rather than partnering with an existing bank):

| Requirement | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| PRA authorisation | Dual-regulated firm (FCA + PRA) |
| Capital requirements | CRD IV / Basel III |
| NNEG reserving (SS3/17) | PRA supervisory statement on equity release valuation |
| Stress testing | PRA annual stress test (if above threshold) |
| Recovery & resolution plan | Required for deposit-takers and significant lenders |

**Recommendation for Phase 1:** Partner with an existing PRA-authorised lender rather than seeking own PRA authorisation. This saves 12-18 months and significant capital.

## 6. Platform & Technology Certifications

### 6.1 Security & Compliance

| Certification | Purpose | Timeline | Cost | Priority |
|--------------|---------|----------|------|----------|
| **SOC 2 Type I** | Security controls attestation (point-in-time) | 3-6 months | $30K-$80K | High — US institutional partners require it |
| **SOC 2 Type II** | Security controls attestation (12-month period) | 12-18 months (after Type I) | $50K-$120K | Required within 18 months of launch |
| **ISO 27001** | Information security management system | 6-12 months | $20K-$50K | High — UK/EU partners expect it |
| **PCI DSS** | Payment card data security | N/A — EPM doesn't handle card data | N/A | Not required |
| **Cyber Essentials (UK)** | Basic cyber security certification | 1-2 months | £300-£500 | Required for UK government-adjacent work |
| **Cyber Essentials Plus (UK)** | Verified cyber security | 2-3 months | £1,500-£5,000 | Recommended |

### 6.2 Recommended Path

```
Month 1-3:   Cyber Essentials (UK) — quick win, low cost
Month 3-6:   SOC 2 Type I — US partners and lenders expect this
Month 6-12:  ISO 27001 — comprehensive, internationally recognised
Month 12-18: SOC 2 Type II — proves sustained compliance
```

### 6.3 Accessibility

| Standard | Requirement | Regions |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| WCAG 2.1 AA | Web accessibility standard | All (legal requirement in US under ADA, UK under Equality Act, AU under DDA) |
| Section 508 | US federal accessibility | US (if any government interaction) |

## 7. Timeline & Dependencies

### 7.1 Critical Path

```
MONTH 1-2: CORPORATE SETUP
├── Company incorporation (each region)
├── ABN/EIN/IRD/Companies House registration
├── Director appointments, fit & proper assessments
└── PI insurance obtained

MONTH 2-4: LICENCE APPLICATIONS FILED
├── AU: ACL + AFSL applications to ASIC
├── US: NMLS registration + state licence applications (CA, FL, AZ, NY)
├── NZ: FSP registration + FAP licence to FMA
├── UK: FCA authorisation application
└── All: Dispute resolution scheme membership

MONTH 3-6: WHILE WAITING FOR LICENCES
├── Staff certifications (RG 146, NMLS exam, NZ Cert, CeMAP/ER01)
├── SOC 2 Type I preparation
├── Cyber Essentials (UK)
├── ERC membership application prepared
├── Product documentation (TMD, PDS, credit guides)
└── AML/CFT program implementation

MONTH 6-9: LICENCES GRANTED (EXPECTED)
├── AU: ACL likely granted (AFSL may take longer)
├── US: FL + AZ licences (faster states)
├── NZ: FAP licence
├── UK: FCA authorisation (may extend to month 12)
└── Begin: SOC 2 Type I audit

MONTH 9-12: REMAINING APPROVALS
├── AU: AFSL granted
├── US: CA + NY licences (slower states)
├── UK: FCA authorisation finalised
├── UK: ERC membership confirmed
├── SOC 2 Type I obtained
└── ISO 27001 implementation underway

MONTH 12+: LAUNCH READINESS
├── All licences obtained
├── All staff certified
├── SOC 2 Type I in hand, Type II period begins
├── Product approved by each regulator
└── GO LIVE
```

### 7.2 Dependencies

| Dependency | Blocks |
|-----------|--------|
| Company incorporation | → All licence applications |
| PI insurance | → ACL, AFSL, FAP, FCA applications |
| Fit & proper assessments | → ACL, AFSL, FCA (responsible managers) |
| Staff certifications | → Licence conditions (can't advise without qualification) |
| AML program | → FSP registration (NZ), NMLS (US), FCA (UK) |
| Dispute resolution membership | → ACL (AU), FSP (NZ) |
| FCA authorisation | → ERC membership application |
