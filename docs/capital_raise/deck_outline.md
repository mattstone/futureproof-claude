# Investor Deck — Core (15) + VC Extension (4)

> **Audience:**
> - **Core deck (slides 1–15):** Used in every meeting. Audience-shared with light tuning notes per slide. Family office principals (Tier 1/2) and fintech-specialist VC partners (Tier 4).
> - **VC extension (E1–E4):** Used in VC meetings. Sits in the appendix for FO meetings — surfaced if a principal asks for depth on exit, expansion, or moat.
>
> **Format:** 16:9, minimal text per slide, one chart or one image per slide max.
>
> **Length rule:** If a slide needs more than 40 words, you haven't decided what it's about.
>
> **Order rule (FO):** Human story → asset class → unit economics → AI ops → ask. Don't reverse.
> **Order rule (VC):** Market structure → product → AI ops → unit economics → exit/expansion → ask. The honest-disclosure slide (E1) goes between AI ops and unit economics.
>
> **Currency convention:** SAFE round is USD-denominated (US-led capital raise via Mike Spidaliere + Max Goldberg). Financial model figures in USD throughout (source-of-truth model is global). Slides that quote model figures footnote: *"Source: Financial Model (USD)."* AU market context figures (e.g. Retirement Income Covenant tailwind A$3.5T) stay in AUD where the regulatory framing demands.
>
> **Numbers source-of-truth:** Every quoted number traces to the Financial Model — Assumptions or P&L tab. If a number on a slide doesn't trace, fix the slide.
>
> **Placeholder convention:** `[ ]` = fill in your specifics before sending.
