## How each email template is meant to be used

The Outreach Pack contains 10 email templates + 4 phone-script sections. Each is purpose-built.

### For Mike + Max running US (VCs)
- **A1 — Warm-intro forward** (175 words). Highest-volume. Mike writes; the connector forwards verbatim. Default when a portfolio founder agrees to relay.
- **A2 — Direct (existing relationship)** (130 words). Mike has worked with the partner before. Lower formality, faster ask.
- **A3 — Cold with specific hook** (165 words). No warm intro, but a portfolio-company or public-statement reference. Use sparingly. Tier-A specialists only.
- **A4 — Post-meeting follow-up** (130 words). Send within 18 hours. Address the specific concern raised in item 1, not generic.
- **A5 — Quarterly update** (180 words). For VCs that pass — ~30% come back next round.

### For you (FOs, AU/global)
- **B1 — Warm-intro forward** (195 words). The default for Tier-1 single FOs — **never cold**. Leads with the human story.
- **B2 — Asking a connector for an intro** (150 words). When you want the connector to actively make the intro, not just forward.
- **B3 — Direct from you** (145 words). Used selectively when you've met the principal at an industry event or have a prior peer connection.
- **B4 — Post-meeting follow-up** (165 words). Within 24 hours. Reference the specific gracious moment from the meeting.
- **B5 — Quarterly update** (165 words). Same logic as A5; warmer tone.

### Phone scripts (Section C)
- **C1** Live opening — 30 seconds; warm and cold variants.
- **C2** Voicemail — 20 seconds. State your name twice (start + end). Always include round size.
- **C3** Q&A drill — 14 common questions × prepared answers. **Print this and keep next to the phone.**
- **C4** Closing the call — the four closing moves + "what never to say".

### Operational notes (Section D)
- 5 cadence rules that never break (e.g., never pitch Tier-1 single FOs cold)
- Subject-line craft (good patterns + avoid list)
- What to attach by stage
- Three things never to say in any email or call
- The honest-disclosure principle — including a verbatim phrase to deploy on every VC first call. **This is the single highest-leverage line in the entire pack.**
