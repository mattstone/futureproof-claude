## Outreach cadence rules

1. **Never approach Tier 1 cold.** Always warm intro. A cold email to a single family office burns the contact for 12 months minimum.
2. **You can approach Tier 2 platforms semi-cold** if you have a credible angle (e.g., "we'd value 15 minutes to share how the Retirement Income Covenant is reshaping the alternatives shelf"), but warm intro converts 5–10× better.
3. **Tier 3 (strategics) — go via known sponsor inside the org.** A cold email to a corporate strategy team rarely lands.
4. **Tier 4 — only after anchor.** Pitching VCs without an anchor signal positions you as desperate and inexperienced.
5. **Never run more than 2 anchor candidates in parallel at "term sheet ready" stage.** Family offices talk. They will find out, and they will both pull.
6. **Quarterly update list:** every "pass" goes on a quarterly investor update list. ~30% of family offices come back for the next round once they've watched you execute for 6–12 months.
