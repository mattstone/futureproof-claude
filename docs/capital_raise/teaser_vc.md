## FutureProof

### Building the Next-Generation of Retirement Income & Funding Products for Every Life Stage.

*Unlocking $25T of locked-up retiree home equity. AI-native SaaS platform. Regulated tailwind. AUS → UK → USA.*

---

**The market structure unlock.**
The 2022 **Retirement Income Covenant** legally requires every Australian super fund to have a retirement income strategy for members. Four years on, **most still don't have a credible solution.** The 2020 Callaghan Review explicitly endorsed home equity release as the answer. We're building the turnkey solution super funds can offer their members — a **B2B2C distribution play with a regulated A$3.5T tailwind** and no incumbent.

**The product.**
The Equity Preservation Mortgage® (EPM) converts retiree home equity into a stable lifetime income — no monthly repayments, no compound interest, no estate erosion. Capital is invested in a structured portfolio (~70% equity / ~30% fixed income) with asymmetric hedge collar (cap +40% / floor -20%); run-off mechanics eliminate prepayment risk to funders. **Validated by 50,000-path Monte Carlo (Futureproof's proprietary financial model):** PoC year-30 = **0.03 (3%)** portfolio capital shortfall probability. Mean per-mortgage surplus year-30: **$1.17M (mean) / $4.10M (99%-ile)** (USD).

**The venture-scale thesis: AI-native operating leverage.**
We are leaning hard into AI to operate a regulated financial-services business at software-company cost — without compromising customer service. **By Y5: 38 FTE running multi-region, multi-billion AUM.** Working prototype: customer-facing AI agent (Claude, 4 regions, proprietary-content guardrails). Built: agent orchestration + 3-type agent infrastructure (applications / backoffice / investment). Funded roadmap (next 12 months): LLM-augmented application triage, ops admin agents, multi-region marketing content, CS triage at scale, investment-ops workflow. **Opex per AUM at Y3: ~14 bps vs 50–80 bps for comparable specialty insurer.** AI doesn't underwrite, doesn't replace compliance, doesn't generate alpha — it absorbs the work *around* regulated decisions. Full breakdown: `ai_architecture.md`.

**Why this is a hard sell for many VCs (read first).**
(1) Specialty-insurance economics, not SaaS — path to 10–50× depends on AUM growth + strategic exit, not ARR multiple expansion. (2) 7–10 year exit window. (3) Capital-stack-dependent — we don't warehouse risk, but we rely on wholesale funder partners. (4) AI is operating leverage, **not** a moat we claim. **If any of these is a hard veto for your fund, please tell us on the first call. We'd rather close the loop fast than burn 4 weeks of process.**
